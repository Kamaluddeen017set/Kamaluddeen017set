The SafeRide Concept

SafeRide's main goal is the efficient organization of passenger transport via taxis and napep in kano state.
It is a booking platform for taxis, private cars and napep, which optimizes the booking process for both,customers and drivers.
With nowadays technology and its intelligent adoption to taxi, private cars and napep booking customers and drivers can profit.
This Win-Win is created by utilizing methods and technologies from the 3mtt software development fellew name kamaluddin Aliyu Ahmad FE/23/3766942 and this can lead to a better route planning and capacity utilization of taxis, cars and napep in kano state.
The driver benefits through higher profits and revenues.
The customer profits through discounts.

With SafeRide the passenger benefits

With SafeRide the passenger gets the complete choice with regards to price, comfort and flexibility. On the one hand he can choose between taxis,private cars and napep and get the right choice for each occasion. On the other hand he can pre-book and get access to discounts or book Taxis ad-hoc and get the known flexibility he used to when booking taxis. On top of the complete choice SafeRide offers approved quality of drivers and vehicles, which is ensured through a quality monitoring system. The passenger can lean back and rely on the competence of the driver and cleanliness of the car. The streamlined booking process via Internet and mobile App as well as the hassle free e-payment and billing process complete the SafeRide offering.
